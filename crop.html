Our Crops

Vegetables

We grow tomatoes, carrots, lettuce, cucumbers, peppers, and onions. All are 100% organic and harvested daily.

Fruits

Our orchards produce apples, strawberries, and blueberries. These are picked at peak ripeness for best flavor.

Seasonal Availability

Spring: lettuce and strawberries. Summer: tomatoes and peppers. Fall: apples and carrots. Winter: stored root vegetables.
